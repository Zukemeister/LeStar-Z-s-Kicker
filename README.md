# LeStar-Z-s-Kicker is an app for your phone that helps you learn where the cueball will go in a game of pool, billiards or snooker. You set your cueball on the table and also on your app, determine where your target is, and use the app to calculate a possible path to get there. It will calculate for spin (English) and for the speed of your shot. If your results consistently are different from what the app tells you, you can build a correction into the app, using the "Bias" slider. Most important: Have Fun!
